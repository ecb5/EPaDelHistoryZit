Michael Fraboni
Advisor: Terrence John Napier
Dissertation Title: Some \(q\)-Convexity Properties of Coverings of Convex Manifolds
was born in Waverly, New York. He earned a BS in Mathematics at he University of Scranton in 1997, his MS and Ph. D. from Lehigh University in 1999 and 2002, respectively.
Michael has been a member of EPaDel since his undergraduate days. While at Scranton he won the EPaDel student paper competition in 1997 for a paper he wrote about work he did with Prof. Ken Monks.
He served as the section's newsletter editor from 2003 to 2006. When the editor position was combined with the newly created webmaster position in 2006 he continued to serve as both until 2012.
in 2002 Michael was hired into the faculty Moravian College in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania. He was promoted to Associate Professor in 2008, and Professor in 20018.
