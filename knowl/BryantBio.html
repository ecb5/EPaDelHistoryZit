Lance Bryant
Ph. D. Advisor:William James Heinzer
Dissertation Title: Filtered Numerical Semigroups and Applications to One-Dimensional Rings
was born in Chillicothe, Ohio (not far from Columbus). He earned his BA in mathematics from Berea College in 2002 with minors in Computer Science and Music. He joined Shippensburg University in 2009 as an Assistant Professor, and was promoted to Associate Professor in 2014.
Lance and his wife Sarah Bryant founded the Shippensburg Area Math Circle for 4th and 5th grade students from 2014 to 2019.
