One of the issues central to the success of SIGs was communication. In an effort to improve overall communication with the entire membership the national MAA office initiated a program of Institutional MAA Representatives in 1977. These volunteers distributed MAA material to colleagues, encouraged the participation of members in sectional and national activities, and recruited new members. The newsletter from the fall 1988 meeting lists EPADEL representatives from 76 different institutions.
