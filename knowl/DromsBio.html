Sean Droms
Ph.D. Adviser: Thomas E. Mark
Dissertation Title: Constructions of Stein Fillings
was born near Harrisonburg, Virginia. He earned his BS in 2007 from the University of Mary Washington, in Fredericksburg, Virginia, and his Ph.D. in 2013 from the University of Virginia, in Charlottesville, Virginia.
Sean joined the math faculty at Lebanon Valley College, in Annville, Pennsylvania in 2013 and was promoted to Associate Professor in 2019.
He has been a member of the section since 2013, serving as a Member-At-Large of the Executive Committee from 2015 to 2018, as Section Historian from 2016 to 2019, as webmaster from 2019 to 2023. He served as the Vice Chair in 2020 and 2021, as as chair of the Executive Committee from 2021 to 2023.
