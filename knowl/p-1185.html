Styer served as EPADEL President (2007-2009), multiple terms as an At-Large member of the Executive Committee, and was a local organizer for section meetings in spring of 1999, fall of 2005, and fall of 2016. He has been active in promoting mathematics and science in the Philadelphia area, having taught a course on the Elementary and Middle School Math Olympiads, coordinated the MAA AMC/AIME exams for homeschoolers and was involved with two NSF Education grants to assist teachers with STEM education training.
