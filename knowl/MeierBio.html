earned his BA from the University of Virginia in 1987, and his MS and Ph.D. from Cornell University in 1990 and 1992, respectively.
John joined Lafayette College in \(1992\text{.}\) He was promoted to Associate Professor in \(1999\text{,}\) to Professor \(2004\text{.}\) In \(2019\) he was became the Provost of Lafayette College. John has been an active member of EPaDel since \(2003\text{,}\) serving as President in \(2005\text{,}\) \(2006\text{,}\) and \(2007\text{.}\) He was also the \(2010\) recipient of the James P. Crawford Teaching Award.
